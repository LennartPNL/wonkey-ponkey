Game

General
  • Press the space bar to launch the ball;
  • Move your racket/rocket towards the ball to hit it;
  • When you hit the ball, and the ball hits the circular border, you are in the scoring zone;
  • If your opponent does not touch the ball, and the ball hits the circular border while you are in the scoring zone, you get a point;
  • May your opponent hit the ball while you are in the scoring zone, you leave the scoring zone;
  • Scoring a goal initiates the start of a new round;
  • The new round begins when the space bar is pressed;
  • The first player to reach 5 points wins the match, if the goal difference is at least 3 points.
Player 1
  • Uses W S A D to move.
Player 1
  • Uses arrow keys on keyboard to move.
Tips
  • Hitting the ball in such a way it rolls along the border almost always results in a point!
  • Hit your opponent to prevent him from hitting the ball!
  • Shield the ball with your racket/rocket!
  • Moving forward is three times as fast as moving backwards!
